    Meet the people behind our magical product and their role

    Pov Chanvannak

    Vice President


    Pov Chanvannak, a dynamic co-founder of Re-Move, brings a wealth of operational expertise to the company's foundation. Hailing from Cambodia, Chanvannak's background in logistics and supply chain management has been instrumental in shaping Re-Move's efficient and reliable moving services. With a passion for optimizing processes and ensuring seamless operations, he has played a pivotal role in establishing the company's strong network of local partnerships and streamlined logistics infrastructure. Chanvannak's dedication to quality customer experiences is evident in his meticulous planning and execution, allowing Re-Move to deliver on its promises of professionalism and transparency. His visionary approach and commitment to innovation continue to drive Re-Move's growth in Cambodia's competitive e-commerce landscape.

    Neth Wandara

    Founder & President


    Neth Wandara, the visionary founder of the Cambodian e-commerce company "Re-Move," has revolutionized the moving service industry through her innovative spirit and commitment to customer well-being. Drawing on her entrepreneurial drive and early experience in online marketplaces, Dara recognized a gap in the market for a tech-driven, customer-centric moving service. Founded in 2015, Re-Move leverages technology to streamline the moving process, providing customers with convenient online booking, real-time updates, and environmentally conscious practices. Dara's focus on social impact is evident in her initiatives to train and employ underprivileged individuals, as well as collaborations with NGOs for community outreach. With a blend of innovation, sustainability, and community involvement, Neth Wandara's leadership has positioned Re-Move as a standout player in Cambodia's e-commerce landscape.

    Phol Sopheaktra

    Lead Designer


    Phol Sopheaktra, the accomplished lead designer at Re-Move, adds a distinct creative edge to the company's ethos. Hailing from Cambodia, Sopheaktra brings a unique blend of local aesthetics and modern design principles to the forefront. With a background in graphic and industrial design, he possesses a keen eye for visual storytelling and functional aesthetics. Sopheaktra's innovative designs have been integral to Re-Move's user-friendly platform, translating the company's values of convenience and transparency into intuitive user interfaces. His dedication to enhancing the customer experience is evident in his meticulous attention to detail, making relocation feel effortless and engaging. As a key contributor to Re-Move's success, Phol Sopheaktra's design sensibilities reflect the company's commitment to excellence and innovation in the moving service industry.
